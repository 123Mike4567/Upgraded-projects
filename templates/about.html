About Me

This is what I do.

Greetings! I am a Kenyan who embarked on a transformative journey to Poland in 2016. Fueled by a scholarship opportunity, I dedicated a year to mastering the intricacies of the Polish language as a prerequisite for my bachelor's program. Overcoming the challenges of studying in a foreign language, I successfully graduated, marking the inception of my ventures beyond borders.

My passion for IT ignited when a friend unveiled a website with my name and picture. From that moment, the allure of coding and web design captivated my curiosity. This experience marked the corner of my self-taught journey into the world of development!

Embracing the power of online learning, I delved into platforms like Udemy and immersive bootcamps, honing my skills and expanding my understanding of various programming languages and web development frameworks. Through dedicated self-study, hands-on projects, and collaboration with fellow enthusiasts, I navigated the intricate landscape of IT.
